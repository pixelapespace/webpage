ABOUT PIXELAPE

PixelApe represents the convergence of nostalgic pixel art aesthetics with **cutting-edge blockchain technology**. Our project draws inspiration from the classic 8-bit and 16-bit gaming era, reimagining digital art ownership for the Web3 generation.


Each PixelApe NFT is a unique digital collectible with **provable scarcity and ownership** secured by the Ethereum blockchain. Our collection features a diverse range of ape characters, from common forest dwellers to legendary golden apes with ultra-rare attributes.


Beyond being digital art pieces, PixelApes serve as **membership tokens** for our growing community. Holders gain exclusive access to community events, future project developments, and special utility within the PixelApe ecosystem we're building.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is PixelApe?
PixelApe is a collection of 10,000 unique pixel art ape NFTs on the Ethereum blockchain. Each NFT is algorithmically generated from over 170 hand-drawn traits, creating a diverse and visually striking collection that pays homage to classic pixel art aesthetics.
How can I mint a PixelApe?
You can mint directly from our website during the public sale. Connect your Ethereum wallet (like MetaMask), ensure you have enough ETH for the mint price and gas fees, and click the mint button. Presale participants will need to be on the whitelist.
What is the utility of owning a PixelApe?
PixelApe holders gain access to exclusive community events, future airdrops, governance rights in our DAO, and special utilities within the PixelApe ecosystem we're building. Ownership also grants commercial rights to your specific PixelApe.
Are there any rarity traits?
Yes! Each PixelApe has varying rarity levels. Some traits like golden fur, laser eyes, crown headwear, and special backgrounds are extremely rare and highly sought after by collectors. The rarity distribution is transparent and verifiable on-chain.
What is the secondary market royalty?
We have set a 5% royalty on all secondary sales. This helps fund ongoing development, community initiatives, marketing efforts, and future project expansions while ensuring the long-term sustainability of the PixelApe ecosystem.
Where can I view my PixelApe after minting?
After minting, your PixelApe will appear in your connected wallet and on major NFT marketplaces like OpenSea. You can also view your collection directly on our website by connecting your wallet to the "My Collection" section.
